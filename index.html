Sylvia

On October 8, 2016, I adopted my daughter: a three-year-old dilute tri-color kitten. This is our journey so far.

Our First Day

Sylvia really hates car rides. After we got home and I let her out of her temporary carrying box, she jumped up on the table and voiced her displeasure. She was perfect.

Majesty

It didn't take long for Sylvia to make herself at home. Everywhere. I'm pretty sure the apartment belongs to her now. I'm just the (occasionally unwelcome) guest.

Some Of Her Favorite Things

Destroying Blinds

There's an outdoor cat who lives next door and comes into our yard a lot. Sylvia's probably just interested in the cat, but I like to think she's watching me, too.

The West Wing

She likes CJ. She has good taste.

Modeling

She is the most handsome.

In Conclusion...

Sylvia is Best Cat and I couldn't be happier that she's with me. Even when she's waking me up at five a.m., and when her breath smells like fish, and when she decides my feet are vermin that must die.

And I'm fairly certain she'd vote me Best, too.
